About Us

WE KNOW MEN

We strive to provide high quality products in a relaxed atmosphere and with help from knowledgeable staff, we make shopping EASY again. Jim’s Clothes Closet was established in Port Alberni 50 years ago and has since grown to 4 locations within the province of British Columbia as well as an online store.


Whether you’re a man who likes to take his time and look around or a guy who wants to be in and out as quick as possible, our knowledgeable staff will be there to help you out. Come visit one of our stores and see how we make shopping enjoyable.
